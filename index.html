About Me

I'm a Web Developer

Hello! I’m Harsh Mandal, a passionate and detail-oriented Web Developer specializing in Frontend Development and UI/UX Design.

I’m currently pursuing my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Manav Rachna International Institute of Research & Studies, where I’m building a strong foundation in programming, web development, and design.Born and raised in Delhi, with roots in Bihar, I’ve always been curious about how websites are built and how code and creativity come together to shape digital experiences. That curiosity turned into a journey when I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and now I enjoy turning ideas into clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites.

Here’s what I currently work with:

Frontend Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Version Control: Git & GitHub

I believe in learning by doing — I spend time building small projects, experimenting with new designs, and expanding my knowledge step-by-step. My goal is to become a full stack web developer, and I’m gradually moving toward backend technologies and frameworks. I’m also working hard to secure an internship opportunity to apply my skills in a real-world environment and gain valuable industry experience during my college years. Thanks for visiting my portfolio! I’m Harsh Mandal, and I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and collaborating.
